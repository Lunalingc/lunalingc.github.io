#1 Blue Exorcist

Blue exorcist is an anime about a boy, Rin Okumura, who is a demon. He and his brother, Yukio Okumura, were adopted by a priest and were raised in a church community. The priests weren't normal though, they were all exorcists, or demon hunters. Rin and Yukio both want to learn the ways of an exorcist and go the school for upcoming exorcists, True Cross Academy. But with Rin being a demon himself a lot of problems come their way, in the form of friends and enemies. Rin doesn't know how to control his powers and requires a lot of help, but not a lot of people trust him.

#2 One Punch Man

Saitama, the unknown hero, a hero who is so strong he can defeat his enemies with just one punch. He thinks his life is boring because no one is a challenge for him. Once he is joined by the cyborg boy, genos, he finds the true meaning of being a hero. The Hero Association is an assocation were heroes are ranked by their deeds. But while it's obvious Saitama is one of the best heroes, because of his fame, he's still not in rank A, or even close to it. But when the Hero Association is under Saitama is the only one who can save them.

#3 Avatar: The Last Airbender

For one hundred years the world has been in danger, the only one who can save it is the avatar. When in some desperate times the siblings, Katara and Sokka find avatar Aang covered in ice, they gave hope to their people. The avatar has to learn to control all of the four elements, but with a banished prince trying to capture Aang it becomes quite hard to find teachers. All hope is lost when the fire nation conquers all the nations but when the prince turns on his family and embraces himself he and the gaang save everyone.
